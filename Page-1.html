How does NanoGen Predict work?

Predicting mRNA Degradation with AI

It is known that mRNA has a tendency to spontaneously degrade, and one cut can render the whole vaccine useless. To help unlock the key to the end of the special freeze storage needed to prevent mRNA degradation, NanoGen Predict uses Recurrent Neural Networks (RNNs) to analyze a mRNA sequence and give a prediction on the degradation rate at every nucleotide base pair. Trained on Eterna's RNA sequence dataset, NanoGen Predict provides the next step to the stabilization of the SARS-CO-V-2 vaccine for global distribution.
